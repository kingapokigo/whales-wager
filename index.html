🐋 How to Play Whale's Wager

1. Name Your Whales.
Each player picks a whale avatar and enters their name. You can add up to 6 players. Once your pod is ready, click "Swim to Start" and let the wagering begin.

2. Roll the Dice & Move Your Whale.
On your turn, roll the dice to move your whale along the ocean track (1–80). The first to reach tile 80 wins the game and ultimate bragging rights 🏆.

3. Land on a Tile? Draw a Card.
After you move, you’ll immediately draw a card. It will either be:

  • 🐋 Whale Card (Good): Fun challenges, dares, trivia, or drinking prompts. Usually harmless. Often hilarious.
  • 🍄 Toxic Mushroom Card (Bad): These are risky. You might lose your next turn, move backwards, or have to complete a physical challenge. Proceed with caution.

4. Card Rules.
Tap the card to flip it and reveal your challenge. Once completed, click "Next Player" to pass the turn. If the card says “Move Back” or “Skip Turn,” consequences are applied automatically.

5. Taking Turns.
The game auto-tracks whose turn it is — just follow the prompt at the top (e.g., “🎯 Kinga’s Turn!”). Don’t try to cheat. The whales are watching 👀.

6. Victory Conditions.
The first whale to reach tile 80 wins Whale’s Wager. There are no second places. Only legends.
