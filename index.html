Welcome to Whale's Wager!

How to Play

Objective: Be the first whale to swim across the board by completing challenges and avoiding toxic traps!

Card Types

Whale Cards: Fun challenges, trivia, or bets. Complete to roll the dice and move forward.

Toxic Mushroom Cards: Watch out! These may make you lose a turn, move backward, or swap spots.

Gameplay

Each player picks a whale piece.

On your turn: Draw a card → Complete challenge → Roll dice (1–6) → Move your whale.

First player to reach the final space wins!

Optional: Keep score, add drinks, or team up for chaos!
