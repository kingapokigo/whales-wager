🐋 Whale's Wager – The Official Gameplay

🎯 Objective:

Be the first player to swim your whale from Start to Finish by completing challenges, dodging traps, and taking hilarious, often absurd risks. There are bonus spaces, trap spaces, and challenge cards (Whale and Toxic Mushroom).

🎲 Setup:

  • 2–6 players choose and name their whales.
  • Shuffle Whale Cards and Toxic Mushroom Cards into a central deck (or two separate decks).
  • Decide your stakes—does the loser do a dare? Does the winner get a prize? It’s up to you.

👣 On Your Turn:

  1. Draw a card (either randomly or from a shuffled combined deck).
  2. 🐋 Whale Card: Fun, silly, or physical challenge.
    🍄 Toxic Mushroom Card: Penalty (move back, skip turn, swap places).
  3. Do the challenge or face the consequence (like skipping your dice roll).
  4. If you complete the challenge, roll a die (1–6) and move your whale forward.
  5. If you land on:
    • Trap space: Draw a Toxic Mushroom card.
    • Bonus space: Roll again or move forward +1 space.

🔥 Card Types (Examples):

🐋 Whale Cards:

  • “Do 10 pushups or take 3 sips.”
  • “Trivia Time! What’s the largest animal in the ocean? Get it right or skip your turn.”
  • “Convince the group you’re a whale. Best impersonation wins a double roll.”

🍄 Toxic Mushroom Cards:

  • “You got beached. Skip your next turn.”
  • “Swap places with the player closest to the finish line.”
  • “You panicked and swam backwards. Move back 2 spaces.”

💥 Twists & Wagers:

  • Group Wagers: At any time, players can start a mini-wager (e.g., “Who can hold a plank the longest?”). Winner gets a bonus roll.
  • Team Betrayals: Players can form temporary alliances… but only for a round or two!
  • Drinking Mode: Add sips or shots in place of certain physical challenges. Proceed with hydration and caution. 🍻

🏁 Winning the Game:

The first whale to reach the final space must land on it with an exact roll. If not, they stay in place. This gives others a chance to catch up and keeps the tension spicy till the last splash.
